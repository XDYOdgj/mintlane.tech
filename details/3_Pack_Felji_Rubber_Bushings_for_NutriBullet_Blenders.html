3 Pack Felji Rubber Bushings Shock Pads for NutriBullet 600W & 900W Blenders

Keep your NutriBullet blender running smoothly and quietly with these high-quality rubber bushings shock pads from Felji. Designed specifically for the NutriBullet 600W and 900W models (NB-101B and NB-101S), these replacement parts help reduce vibrations and noise, ensuring a more efficient and enjoyable blending experience. Whether you're making smoothies, juices, or sauces, these shock pads provide the stability your blender needs to perform at its best. Note: This product is not compatible with Baby Bullet, Magic Bullet MB1001, NutriBullet RX or Nutri Ninja blenders. Each pack includes three durable rubber bushings, featuring dimensions of 0.62" (height) x 1" (length) x 0.57" (width), perfect for easy installation and long-lasting performance. A must-have for any NutriBullet blender owner looking to maintain the quality and longevity of their kitchen appliance.
