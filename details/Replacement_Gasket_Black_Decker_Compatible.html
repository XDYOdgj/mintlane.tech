Replacement Gasket for Black & Decker Blenders

Designed to fit a variety of Black & Decker blender models, this high-quality silicone rubber gasket is your reliable solution for sealing and durability. Perfect for maintaining smooth operation and preventing leaks, it ensures your kitchen appliance remains in top condition.

This gasket is compatible with models such as BL1900, BL1900R, BL2900, BL3900SKT, BL4900, BL5000, BL9000, and BLKT5950C. With an inside diameter of 2" (25.4mm) and an outside diameter of 2.63" (66.8mm), it fits securely and functions efficiently in your blender.

Each package includes two gaskets, allowing you to have a spare on hand. Please note that this gasket is not compatible with model B19000. This is an aftermarket part, not an original equipment manufacturer (OEM) item. All brand names and trademarks are used for reference purposes only.
