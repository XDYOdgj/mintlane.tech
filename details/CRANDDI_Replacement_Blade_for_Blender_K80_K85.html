CRANDDI Replacement Blade for Blender K80 & K85

Ensure optimal blending performance with our high-quality, durable spare blade designed specifically for the CRANDDI K80 and K85 models. Perfect for maintaining your kitchen appliance's efficiency and longevity.

Why Choose This Replacement Blade?

Our CRANDDI spare blades are engineered to deliver smooth and powerful blending results. Made from premium stainless steel, they are built to last and perform reliably under daily use.

Each blade comes with a complete assembly that features a twin bearing and double sealed design. This ensures better blending performance and helps extend the blade's lifespan.
