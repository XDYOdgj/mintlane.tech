Professional 6-Piece Rubber Gaskets for Nutri Ninja Blender

Ensure your Nutri Ninja Blender operates seamlessly with our high-quality replacement gaskets. Perfect for maintenance and long-term use, these gaskets are designed to fit a variety of model numbers and provide reliable sealing performance.

Key Features

  • Size Precision: Diameter 3.94 inches (10 cm), Height 0.4 inches (1 cm), Thickness 0.11 inches (0.3 cm) for optimal fit and function.
  • Seamless Installation: Pre-press the new gasket to ensure smooth placement and prevent leaks. Always remove the old gasket and check for any foreign particles in the blade gap.
  • Time and Cost Efficient: Replace seals to avoid costly repairs and maintain efficient performance. Our gaskets are a practical solution for everyday use.

Compatibility

  • Ninja Pro Extractor Blade Assembly: BL456-30, BL480, BL480D, BL487T, CT680, BL640, BL680, BL681A, BL682, BL910
  • Ninja Auto-iQ Pro Extractor: BL456-30, BL480, BL480D, BL487T
  • Ninja Auto-iQ Smooth Boost Blender: BL490, BL491, BL492, BL492W, BL493Z, BL494
  • Ninja Duo with Auto IQ: NN-100, NN-100A, NN-101, NN-102
  • Ninja Auto IQ Pro Complete Personal Blender: BL486CO, BL487, BL487A, BL487QH
  • Also fits for 18, 24, 32-Oz Blender Cups.

Important Notes

  • Installation Orientation: Always install the gasket with the 1 groove side facing down and the 3 groove side outward to ensure proper fit.
  • Not Suitable For: BL450-70, BL451-70, BL454-70, BL455-70, BL480-70, BL480W-70, BL481-70, BL482-70, BL483-70, BL484-70, Ninja Pro BL481, QB3001SS.
