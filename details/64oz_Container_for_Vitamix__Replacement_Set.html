TASUCY 64oz Container for Vitamix - Container and Blade Assembly

Elevate your blending experience with TASUCY's durable and stylish 64oz container. Perfectly crafted for use with Vitamix models and compatible with a range of popular blending systems.

Key Features

  • Manufactured from high-impact plastic, ensuring long-lasting performance.
  • BPA-free construction for safe use in your kitchen.
  • Clear polycarbonate allows for easy visibility of your ingredients while blending.
  • Equipped with a black rubber handle that provides a secure and comfortable grip.

Measuring 11'' X 9'' X 6.3'', this container is designed to fit seamlessly into your Vitamix setup. It is compatible with the following models: 4500, 5000, 5200, 6000, 6300, VM0101, VM0102, VMO103, TurboBlend, and VMO115A.
