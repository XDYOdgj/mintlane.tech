Blendin 5 Cup Square Top Glass Jar Replacement Part

Perfectly crafted for Oster and Osterizer blenders, this 4" square top glass jar is a reliable upgrade or replacement for your kitchen appliance.

Key Features
  • Sturdy, high-quality glass construction
  • 4" diameter opening for easy ingredient access
  • Designed to fit Oster models with a small square pin on the motor cap
  • Compatible with blenders having a base blade diameter of 2 5/8" and motor top opening of 3 3/8"
  • Does not fit Oster Pro or Versa models
  • Easy to install and use for everyday blending needs
Why Choose This Jar?

This replacement glass jar is ideal for home cooks and busy families who need a durable and efficient blender accessory. Its 4" opening makes it easy to add ingredients without struggle, and the square top design ensures a secure fit with compatible models. Whether you're making smoothies, soups, or sauces, this jar enhances your blending experience with a reliable and stylish finish.

Important Notes

Please verify your blender's dimensions before purchasing. This jar is compatible with models where the motor opening is no larger than 3 3/8" and the blade base has a 2 5/8" diameter. It may not fit Oster Pro or Versa series blenders.
